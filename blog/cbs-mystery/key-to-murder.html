"Key to Murder," a masterfully crafted episode of CBS Radio Mystery Theater, aired on March 28, 1975. The story revolves around James Parker, a renowned cryptologist who returns to his hometown of New Haven after a long absence. Upon his arrival, he becomes embroiled in a mystery surrounding the murder of a local businessman and the theft of a valuable artifact – an ancient key that could unlock the secrets of a centuries-old city.

As Parker delves deeper into the case, he must navigate a complex web of relationships and alliances, all while confronting his own troubled past. The tension builds as the stakes grow higher, culminating in a shocking climax that will keep listeners on the edge of their seats until the very end.
