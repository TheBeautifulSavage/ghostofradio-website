As our protagonists piece together the puzzle, you'll hear the crackle of tension mount with each clue, each accusation, each revelation that someone very close to them might be harboring deadly secrets. *Bold Venture* emerged in 1951 as a breath of sophisticated danger in the golden age of radio adventure. Settle into your chair as evening falls, and prepare yourself for thirty minutes of Caribbean intrigue that keeps you guessing until the final moments.
