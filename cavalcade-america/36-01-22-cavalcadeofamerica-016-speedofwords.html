Since its debut in 1935, Cavalcade of America has been America's premier historical drama series, bringing authentic, carefully researched stories of American achievement directly into the living rooms of forty million listeners. Sponsored by DuPont, the show has become a cultural touchstone, demonstrating that history isn't merely dusty textbooks and distant figures—it's the thrilling saga of ordinary Americans accomplishing the extraordinary. Episodes like "Speed of Words" showcase the program's commitment to celebrating the inventors, builders, and dreamers who literally wired our nation together, making abstract progress tangible and deeply human.
