CBS Radio Mystery Theater* was a masterclass in reviving a dying art form. Premiering in 1974 when television had seemingly dealt radio drama its death blow, the show proved that audiences still craved the intimacy and imagination that only audio storytelling could provide. Though *Deadly Dilemma* aired during the show's golden years in the early 1980s, it embodies the program's commitment to crafting intelligent, morally complex mysteries populated by flawed protagonists and unexpected twists. With stellar supporting performances and a script that rewards careful listening, this episode exemplifies why the series earned legions of devoted fans and won multiple Emmy and Peabody Awards.
