EPISODE SUMMARY:

"Windandingo" is a haunting episode of CBS Radio Mystery Theater that delves into the mysterious disappearance of a wealthy businessman and his family from their rural estate. The story follows private investigator Jack McCann as he becomes embroiled in a web of deceit, blackmail, and small-town secrets. As McCann digs deeper, he uncovers a tangled thread of motives and alibis that lead him to question the true nature of the Windandingo family's existence.

Through the lens of this enigmatic mystery, the writers of CBS Radio Mystery Theater masterfully weave together themes of isolation, paranoia, and the darker side of human nature. The characters are expertly crafted, with each one hiding secrets behind a mask of polite deception. The script is replete with clever plot twists and red herrings, keeping listeners on the edge of their seats as they try to unravel the tangled threads of the mystery.

As McCann navigates the treacherous landscape of small-town politics and family dynamics, he must confront his own biases and assumptions about the people around him. Will he uncover the truth behind the Windandingo disappearance, or will he become the next victim of their sinister game?
