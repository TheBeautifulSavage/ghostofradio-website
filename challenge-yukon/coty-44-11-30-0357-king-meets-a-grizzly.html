# King Meets A Grizzly

Deep in the frozen wilderness where the Yukon stretches endlessly beneath star-studded skies, Sergeant Preston and his magnificent sled dog King face their most formidable adversary yet—a massive grizzly bear driven to savage desperation by hunger and territorial rage. As snow crunches beneath paws and hooves, listeners will be transported to a moment of primal conflict where survival depends on quick thinking, steadfast loyalty, and the unbreakable bond between man and dog. The tension builds as Preston realizes King has wandered into the beast's path, and the loyal husky must draw upon every ounce of courage and instinct to protect his master and himself. This thrilling encounter captures everything that made radio drama magical: the sound of wind howling across the tundra, the menacing growl of a cornered predator, and the heart-pounding realization that in the North, nature itself is the ultimate law.

For over a decade, *Challenge of the Yukon* captivated millions of Americans who gathered around their radio sets to follow Sergeant Preston's adventures in Canada's frozen frontier. Premiering in 1938, the series became a cultural phenomenon, featuring authentic tales of law enforcement, survival, and moral courage set against a landscape both beautiful and merciless. King, the lead sled dog, became as beloved as any human character in radio drama—a character so iconic that the show's later television adaptation would make him a household name.

Tonight, experience why audiences of the 1940s couldn't wait for their next appointment with Sergeant Preston and King. Tune in as the North's finest protectors face nature's fury in "King Meets A Grizzly," where courage, companionship, and the indomitable human spirit prove stronger than any wild force.
