# Guilty by Proxy

The frozen wastes of the Yukon have never been more treacherous than when justice itself becomes the weapon. In this gripping episode of *Challenge of the Yukon*, Sergeant Preston faces his most insidious case yet—a crime where the guilty party hides behind an innocent face, and every clue points to the wrong man. As the bitter Arctic wind howls across the frozen tundra, our stalwart Mountie must unravel a web of deception that threatens to condemn an honest soul to the gallows. With King, his loyal husky companion, at his side, Preston pursues leads through snowbound settlements and isolated trading posts, where the only currency is survival and secrets run as deep as the permafrost beneath their feet. The tension mounts as circumstantial evidence piles like snow drifts, and Preston must race against time before an innocent man pays the ultimate price for another's sin.

*Challenge of the Yukon* stands as one of radio's most enduring adventure serials, captivating audiences for nearly two decades with tales of frontier justice and wilderness intrigue. The show's authentic atmosphere—born from meticulous sound design and scripts rooted in the real dangers of Canada's far north—made listeners feel the bite of Arctic cold through their radio speakers. Episodes like "Guilty by Proxy" showcase the series' trademark blend of detective work and outdoor adventure, proving that danger lurks not just in nature's fury, but in the human heart.

Don't miss this classic tale of misdirected justice and dogged determination. Tune in to *Challenge of the Yukon* and experience the golden age of radio adventure, where honor and truth triumph against impossible odds in the frozen frontier.
