# Challenge of the Yukon: Back Door Of The Mountain

As the theme song's distinctive bugle call pierces the static, Sergeant Preston and his magnificent dog King arrive at a remote mountain pass where danger lurks in every shadow. In "Back Door of the Mountain," our hero discovers that smugglers have been using an obscure northern route to transport contraband across the frozen frontier, and only Preston's keen detective skills and King's extraordinary tracking ability stand between lawlessness and order. The bitter Yukon wind howls through the broadcast as Preston finds himself pursuing criminals through treacherous terrain, where a single misstep means certain death in the unforgiving wilderness. Listeners will grip their radio sets as Preston races against time, knowing that these smugglers will stop at nothing to protect their secret passage—and that discovery of the mountain's hidden "back door" may be his only chance to catch them before they disappear into the vast northern night.

For over a decade, *Challenge of the Yukon* captivated American audiences with tales of frontier justice and adventure, making Sergeant Preston a household name and King a canine celebrity rivaling even Rin Tin Tin. Broadcast during radio's golden age, the show perfectly captured the public's fascination with untamed wilderness and heroic law enforcement during an era when the Canadian Northwest still represented the last great frontier. The program's meticulous attention to authentic detail—from period-accurate dialogue to genuine descriptions of Arctic survival—elevated it beyond simple adventure fare into compelling drama that educated listeners while thrilling them.

Tune in tonight to experience the roar of the Yukon, the loyalty of a heroic dog, and the unwavering courage of a man dedicated to bringing justice to the frozen North. *Challenge of the Yukon* awaits—are you ready to answer?
