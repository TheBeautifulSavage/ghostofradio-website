(0821) The Masked Gunman

The frozen silence of the Yukon wilderness shatters as Sergeant Preston and King thunder across the snow-laden tundra in pursuit of a mysterious outlaw terrorizing the remote trading posts of the North. A gunman shrouded in shadow and deception has struck again—his identity unknown, his motives unclear—leaving a trail of stolen furs and unanswered questions in his wake. As the sergeant closes in with his faithful husky companion at his side, he discovers that nothing in this case is quite as it seems. With danger lurking behind every snow-laden spruce and betrayal lurking in the hearts of trusted friends, Preston must unravel the mystery of the masked gunman's true identity before another shipment vanishes into the wilderness—or worse, before the outlaw's vendetta turns deadly.

Challenge of the Yukon captured the American imagination during the golden age of radio by offering audiences an escape to the untamed frontiers of Canada's frozen north, where civilization was fragile, justice was personal, and adventure lurked in every shadow. Sergeant Preston became a beloved figure—the moral compass of a lawless land—while King, the magnificent German Shepherd, became an icon of loyalty and heroism in his own right. The show's meticulous sound design, featuring authentic dog sled bells and crackling campfire ambience, transported listeners directly into the harsh beauty of the Yukon, making each episode feel urgent and immediate.

Settle in with a warm blanket and hot cocoa as you join Sergeant Preston in this gripping 1940s adventure. Will Preston unmask the gunman before it's too late? Tune in now to discover whether justice prevails in the frozen reaches of Canada's greatest frontier.
