# Out Of The Night

Picture the frozen Yukon wilderness on a moonless evening, where the howl of sled dogs cuts through the endless dark and danger lurks in every snowdrift. In this gripping episode, Sergeant Preston faces one of his most perilous cases yet—a mystery that emerges literally out of the night when a desperate fugitive stumbles into civilization half-frozen and delirious, clutching a secret that could mean life or death for an innocent man. As Preston interrogates the stranger, the full scope of a desperate crime unfolds, and our resourceful Mountie must race against time and the unforgiving elements to separate truth from desperate lies. With loyal King at his side and justice as his only compass, Preston ventures into the black Yukon night on a manhunt that will test his courage, his instincts, and his unwavering commitment to the law.

*Challenge of the Yukon* captured radio audiences' hearts for seventeen thrilling years with its perfect formula of rugged adventure, moral clarity, and the authentic atmosphere of Canada's frozen north. The show arrived during the Golden Age of radio drama, when families huddled around their sets for weekly escapades that transported them to the dangerous frontier. Sergeant Preston—based on the legendary Royal Canadian Mounted Police—embodied the virtues listeners craved: courage, fairness, and an unshakeable dedication to duty. The crisp sound effects of barking dogs, creaking sleds, and howling wind became as beloved as the character himself, while the stirring theme music signaled that justice would prevail, even in the harshest corner of the continent.

Tune in now to experience the suspense, the atmosphere, and the timeless adventure that kept millions spellbound every week. *Out Of The Night* showcases why *Challenge of the Yukon* remains an unforgettable classic of radio's golden era.
