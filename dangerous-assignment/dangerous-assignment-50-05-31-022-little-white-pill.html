What made *Dangerous Assignment* such compelling radio entertainment was its commitment to authentic international intrigue during an era when the world felt simultaneously vast and claustrophobic. Featuring the distinctive voice of Brian Donlevy as the unflappable Steve Mitchell, each episode transported listeners to exotic locales—from Istanbul to Rio de Janeiro—while exploring the moral ambiguities facing those caught between superpowers.
