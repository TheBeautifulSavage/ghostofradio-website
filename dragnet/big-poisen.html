EPISODE SUMMARY:

The air is thick with tension as Sergeant Joe Friday and his trusty sidekick, Officer Bill Gannon, arrive at a posh nightclub to investigate a mysterious poison. The patrons are abuzz with whispers of a deadly concoction that's been circulating among the upper crust, and it seems no one can be trusted. As Friday and Gannon begin to question the club's owner, a nervous energy permeates the air, making even the most composed individuals squirm with unease. It's clear that this is no ordinary case of theft or assault – something far more sinister is at play.

As the investigation deepens, Friday and Gannon uncover a complex web of deceit and betrayal, each new revelation revealing more shocking details about the club's inner circle. The atmosphere is one of creeping dread, as if the very fabric of society is unraveling before their eyes. The iconic "Just the facts, ma'am" refrain takes on an added layer of urgency, underscoring the gravity of the situation and Friday's unyielding dedication to justice.

Meanwhile, a sense of unease lingers beneath the surface, as the listeners are left to wonder: who is behind this sinister plot? Is it a disgruntled employee seeking revenge, or something more complex – perhaps a deliberate attempt to poison the elite in order to further some twisted social agenda? The Big Poison episode masterfully ratchets up the suspense, culminating in a shocking reveal that will leave listeners gasping for air.
