"Casting the Runes" is an episode of Escape, a show that masterfully crafted suspenseful tales for listeners to unravel alongside its protagonists. In this particular episode, Dr. Edward Marston finds himself trapped in a German prison camp, with no memory of how he arrived there. As he tries to piece together his past, he discovers a cryptic message etched into the wall: "Casting the runes." The phrase becomes an eerie mantra for Dr. Marston, as he attempts to decipher its meaning and escape the clutches of his captors.

The atmosphere in this episode is thick with tension, as Dr. Marston's desperate attempts to uncover the truth create a sense of urgency that draws the listener in. The sound design is particularly effective, with eerie echoes and distant sounds that evoke the harsh realities of war-torn Europe. As Dr. Marston navigates his precarious situation, the audience is left wondering what secrets he'll uncover next, and whether he'll be able to escape alive.

The character of Dr. Marston is a complex one, driven by a mix of fear, determination, and curiosity. As he delves deeper into the mystery, the listener finds themselves invested in his fate, willing him to succeed against all odds.
