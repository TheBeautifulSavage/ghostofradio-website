As he searches for his missing colleague, Peter must rely on his wits and cunning to stay one step ahead of his pursuers.

As the episode progresses, we learn that Peter's search is not just about finding his colleague, but also about unraveling the mystery behind a valuable treasure that has gone missing. The sun-baked streets of Marrakech seem to hold secrets and dangers at every turn, and Peter must be constantly on guard to avoid being caught by the bandits. The dramatic music swells as Peter narrowly escapes danger, his heart pounding in his chest.

Meanwhile, back in the United States, Peter's wife, Elizabeth, is left to cope with her husband's disappearance, receiving cryptic messages from Peter that hint at the danger he is in. As the hours tick by, Elizabeth becomes increasingly anxious, unsure of whether she will ever see her beloved husband again. The episode builds to a thrilling conclusion as Peter finally uncovers the truth behind the treasure and his colleague's disappearance.
