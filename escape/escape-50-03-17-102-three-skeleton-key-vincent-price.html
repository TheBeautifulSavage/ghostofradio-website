EPISODE SUMMARY:

"The Three Skeleton Key" is a gripping tale of espionage and betrayal, starring Vincent Price as Reginald Armstrong, an American engineer who becomes embroiled in a web of international intrigue. The story begins with Reg's discovery of a mysterious key hidden in his home, which sets him on a perilous journey to unravel the truth behind the Soviet Union's latest technological advancements. As he delves deeper into the world of espionage, Reg finds himself caught between two rival spies, each with their own sinister agenda.

As the episode unfolds, the atmosphere becomes increasingly tense, with the threat of discovery hanging precariously in the balance. The listener is drawn into a world of smoke-filled rooms, secret meetings, and double-crosses, where the lines between loyalty and deception are constantly blurred. Vincent Price brings his signature style to the role, injecting a sense of gravitas and menace that elevates the episode's suspenseful narrative.

Meanwhile, the supporting cast adds depth and nuance to the story, with characters like Reg's wife, Sarah (played by Martha Vickers), struggling to maintain her own secrets in the face of increasing danger. The writing is masterfully paced, with each scene building on the last to create a sense of momentum that propels the listener forward, eager to uncover the shocking truth behind the three skeleton key.
