"A Bullet for Mr. Smith" is an episode of Escape, a CBS radio drama that premiered in 1947 and ran for eight years. The atmosphere of tension builds as the police navigate a complex web of clues and suspects, all while keeping Henry's fate uncertain.

The episode is notable for its portrayal of the growing unease in post-war America. The year is unknown, but it was a time of increasing Cold War tensions and fears about communism.
