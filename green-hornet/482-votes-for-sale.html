EPISODE SUMMARY:

In "Votes for Sale," a gripping episode of The Green Hornet, Kato and Britt Reid find themselves entangled in a complex web of corruption and deceit. The story begins with the mysterious death of a wealthy businessman, whose body is discovered in an alleyway with no discernible signs of foul play. As Kato and Britt delve deeper into the case, they unravel a sinister plot that threatens to undermine the democratic process. With its intricate plot twists and high-stakes action, this episode embodies the tension and uncertainty of 1940 America.

As the Green Hornet duo navigates the treacherous underworld of corruption and crime, they must confront their own vulnerabilities. Britt's keen intellect is put to the test as he tries to unravel the mystery, while Kato's loyalty and trustworthiness are called into question by those around him. The episode's atmospheric sound design, featuring a haunting saxophone riff and pulsating drums, perfectly captures the sense of unease and foreboding that pervades this gripping tale.
