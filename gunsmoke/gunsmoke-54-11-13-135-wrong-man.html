It's unclear exactly when this episode originally aired, but given its thematic concerns, it likely took place sometime in the late 1950s or early 1960s. By presenting intricate mysteries and nuanced character studies, shows like Gunsmoke provided a much-needed respite from the harsh realities of the time.
