# Hopalong Cassidy: The Mystery at Three Oaks

Picture this: the December wind howls across the Texas badlands as Hopalong Cassidy rides hard toward the sprawling Three Oaks ranch, where a sinister mystery awaits. Livestock has vanished without a trace, ranch hands speak in hushed, fearful whispers, and the local sheriff seems powerless to stop the strange disappearances. With only his wits, his trusty Colt .45, and the loyal support of his crew, Hoppy must unravel a web of deception that runs deeper than anyone suspects. Will he discover whether this is the work of rustlers, sabotage—or something far more sinister lurking in the shadows of Three Oaks? Tune in as our hero faces danger at every turn, where trust is a luxury he can ill afford.

For over a decade, *Hopalong Cassidy* captivated American listeners with tales of justice and adventure in the Old West, bringing William Boyd's iconic character to life through sterling performances and expertly crafted scripts. What made this show exceptional was its refusal to treat the western genre as mere entertainment for children—these were sophisticated crime dramas set against the frontier, where moral ambiguity often proved as threatening as any outlaw's gun. The show's popularity was so immense that it transcended radio, eventually spawning a successful film and television career for Boyd himself. This particular episode, from the show's peak years, represents everything fans loved: genuine suspense, authentic characterization, and the timeless appeal of a hero who always stood on the side of right.

The mystery at Three Oaks awaits those brave enough to saddle up. Don't miss this thrilling installment of *Hopalong Cassidy*—your evening of classic adventure begins now.
