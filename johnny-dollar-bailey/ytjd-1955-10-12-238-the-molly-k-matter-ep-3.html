# The Molly K Matter - Part Three

The salt-stained decks of the fishing vessel *Molly K* hold secrets that insurance investigator Johnny Dollar is determined to unearth. As our hero ventures deeper into the third installment of this compelling case, the fog-shrouded harbor becomes a maze of shadowy suspects and suspicious claims. A ship, a disappearance, and a fortune in salvage rights hang in the balance—and someone aboard that vessel has every reason to see Johnny silenced before he can piece together the truth. With Bob Bailey's distinctive voice cutting through the static like a knife through darkness, listeners are drawn into a world where greed and desperation clash against one man's dogged pursuit of justice. The tension builds with every interview, every clue, every wrong turn down a dockside alley.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* stands as one of radio's most sophisticated crime dramas, and the 1955-1960 CBS era represents the show at its absolute peak. Bailey's portrayal of the expense-account-obsessed yet morally uncompromising investigator became iconic, transforming what could have been a simple procedural into a nuanced exploration of American capitalism and personal integrity. These episodes, recorded during the golden twilight of network radio, capture an irreplaceable moment when millions of Americans gathered around their sets each evening, hanging on every word of Johnny's investigations. The show's attention to realistic detail—from insurance terminology to gritty urban settings—lent it a credibility that separated it from more fantastic contemporaries.

Don't miss the conclusion to this maritime mystery. Tune in and discover whether Johnny Dollar will crack *The Molly K Matter* or if the fog will claim one more victim before the case is closed.
