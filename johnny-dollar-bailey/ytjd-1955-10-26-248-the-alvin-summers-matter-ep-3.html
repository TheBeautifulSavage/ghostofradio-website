# The Alvin Summers Matter (Part 3)

Step into the fog-wrapped streets of a mystery that refuses to let go. In this gripping third installment, Johnny Dollar finds himself deeper in the Alvin Summers case than ever before—the kind of case that follows you home and keeps you awake at three in the morning. With each new lead, the threads grow more tangled, the suspects more desperate, and the truth more elusive. Bob Bailey's weary, world-worn voice guides you through darkened offices and dangerous encounters, where every telephone call could be a trap and every witness might be a liar. The tension builds like a cigarette burning down to the filter, and just when you think you've grasped the answer, the ground shifts beneath your feet. This is insurance investigation as noir poetry—no gunshots or car chases needed when a man's quiet determination and sharp mind are weapons enough.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* stands as one of radio's greatest achievements precisely because it refuses the easy thrills. Premiering in this golden age of CBS Radio, the show built its reputation on meticulous scripts, layered mysteries, and Bailey's incomparable performance—a man who could convey years of cynicism and hidden decency in a single pause. The Alvin Summers matter, running across multiple episodes, showcases the show's willingness to let stories breathe, to let listeners sit with ambiguity and complexity the way real investigators must.

Don't miss this pivotal chapter in one of radio's finest noir dramas. Tune in and discover why *Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* remains the gold standard of detective radio—where the real mystery isn't just solving the crime, but understanding the people caught in its web.
