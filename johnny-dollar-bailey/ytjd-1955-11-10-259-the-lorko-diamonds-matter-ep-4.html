# Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar: The Lorko Diamonds Matter, Episode 4

The fog rolls thick over the docks as insurance investigator Johnny Dollar finds himself deeper in a web of international intrigue, counterfeit identities, and enough suspicion to fill a ledger twice over. In this fourth installment of "The Lorko Diamonds Matter," our world-weary hero must navigate the murky waters between jealous lovers, dangerous criminals, and the shadowy figures who always seem to be one step ahead. With only his quick wit, sharper instincts, and the occasional well-placed telephone call to keep him alive, Johnny closes in on the truth—but each answer only spawns deadlier questions. The sounds of a bustling 1955 urban landscape crackle through your radio speaker: the screech of car brakes, the ominous click of a revolver's safety, urgent whispers in darkened hotel rooms. What really happened to the Lorko diamonds, and who's willing to kill to keep that secret buried?

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* stands as one of the final masterpieces of radio's golden age, and Bob Bailey's portrayal of the titular investigator became legendary for its naturalistic, rapid-fire delivery that perfectly captured the paranoia and exhaustion of a man making his living by asking dangerous people uncomfortable questions. At a time when television was beginning to steal radio's audience, this show proved that the medium's power lay not in what you could see, but in what your imagination could conjure from expert sound design, compelling dialogue, and Bailey's magnetizing voice work.

Don't miss your chance to step into the shadowy world of mid-century insurance fraud and criminal conspiracy. Tune in now and discover why discerning listeners kept Johnny Dollar company through the darkness, night after night.
