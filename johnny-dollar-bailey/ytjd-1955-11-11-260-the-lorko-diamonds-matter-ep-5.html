# The Lorko Diamonds Matter – Part Five

The fifth installment of "The Lorko Diamonds Matter" finds insurance investigator Johnny Dollar deep in treacherous waters, pursuing a trail of precious stones that seems to multiply suspects faster than it yields answers. In this episode, the atmosphere crackles with the kind of noir tension that made Bob Bailey's portrayal of the wisecracking, quick-thinking detective essential listening for millions of Americans crowded around their radios on Friday nights. Rival claims collide, alibis crumble under scrutiny, and Johnny finds himself caught between powerful interests who'd prefer he abandon his investigation altogether. The snappy dialogue and intricate plotting will keep you guessing right up until the final revelation, as Bailey delivers his characteristic blend of hardboiled cynicism and genuine integrity.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* stands as a remarkable achievement in radio drama—a show that ran for five years on CBS precisely because it understood what made noir thrill audiences: not just danger and intrigue, but a protagonist you could trust completely, even when everyone else seemed bent on deception. The "Lorko Diamonds" multi-part case exemplifies the show's ambitious storytelling, unfolding across five episodes with the narrative complexity of a serialized mystery novel. Bob Bailey brought a naturalistic, conversational quality to radio acting that made Johnny Dollar feel less like a fictional character and more like a friend recounting his latest case over a cigarette and whiskey.

Don't miss this crucial chapter in one of radio's most enduring detective series. Tune in to hear how Johnny Dollar navigates the shadowy world of high-stakes diamond theft, where everyone lies and only the most observant—or the most fortunate—walk away unscathed.
