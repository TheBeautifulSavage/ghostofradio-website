# Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar: The Nick Shurn Matter (Episode 1)

When Johnny Dollar steps into the murky underworld of the Nick Shurn case on this December evening in 1955, listeners will find themselves drawn into one of the most intricate webs of deception ever spun across the airwaves. The first installment of this multi-part investigation crackles with tension as our man from the insurance company uncovers layer after layer of mystery—each revelation more dangerous than the last. Bob Bailey's distinctive voice cuts through the noir atmosphere with the weary confidence of a seasoned investigator who's seen it all, yet expects nothing. The snap of a lighter, the clink of ice in a glass, and the ever-present hum of the city at night form the perfect backdrop as Johnny begins piecing together a puzzle that will keep him—and you—sleepless until the final truth emerges.

By 1955, *Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* had already established itself as radio's preeminent insurance investigator drama, a show that brought hard-boiled detective fiction into the world of claims and fraud with remarkable authenticity. What set this series apart was its meticulous attention to detail and its willingness to stretch stories across multiple episodes, a daring format that kept audiences returning night after night. Bailey's portrayal of Dollar became the gold standard for the genre—not a superhero, but a thinking man's detective navigating a world of temptation and corruption.

This particular episode marks the beginning of one of the series' most celebrated cases. Whether you're a devoted fan of classic radio mysteries or a newcomer to Johnny Dollar's world, *The Nick Shurn Matter* promises the kind of storytelling that made radio's golden age truly golden. Tune in and discover why millions turned their dials to follow this remarkable investigator into the shadows.
