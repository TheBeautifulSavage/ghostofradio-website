# Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar: The Forbes Matter (Part 3)

The winter fog rolls thick through the streets of San Francisco as insurance investigator Johnny Dollar finds himself entangled in a web of betrayal and hidden motives. In this gripping third installment of *The Forbes Matter*, our hardboiled hero must navigate the treacherous underworld of society blackmail and corporate espionage. With each clue uncovered, the stakes grow higher—and someone is willing to silence Johnny permanently to keep their secrets buried. Bob Bailey's distinctive, world-weary narration guides listeners through shadowy hotel corridors and dangerous midnight rendezvous, where trust is a luxury no one can afford. Every dollar of Johnny's expense account may be his last.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* stands as a testament to the golden age of radio drama, where CBS brought listeners the most sophisticated and gritty crime stories of the 1950s. Bob Bailey's five-year tenure in the title role defined the character with his sharp wit and cynical wisdom, creating one of broadcasting's most beloved detectives. This episode, originally aired December 28, 1955, exemplifies the show's masterful three-part story structure, allowing complex narratives to unfold with the pacing of a serialized pulp novel. The program was meticulously crafted, with each episode featuring Johnny's memorable expense account recitation—a distinctive touch that made listeners feel like intimate confidants in his dangerous profession.

Tune in for *The Forbes Matter* conclusion and experience radio drama at its finest. This is the sound of noir come alive, where every word crackles with tension and mystery.
