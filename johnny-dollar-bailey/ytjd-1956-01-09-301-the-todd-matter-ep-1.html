# The Todd Matter, Episode 1

On a cold January evening in 1956, tune in as insurance investigator Johnny Dollar steps into one of his most labyrinthine cases—*The Todd Matter*. Bob Bailey's smooth, world-weary narration guides you through the fog-laden streets where nothing is quite as it seems. A routine claim becomes anything but routine when Johnny encounters tangled motives, shadowy figures, and a mystery that will demand all his cunning to unravel. As the first episode unfolds, you'll find yourself drawn into a web of deceit where trust is a luxury no one can afford. The snap of dialogue, the ominous musical cues, and Bailey's distinctive deadpan delivery create an atmosphere thick with suspense—this is classic noir brought to vivid life through your radio speaker.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* represents the golden age of radio drama at its finest. CBS's five-minute-episode format was revolutionary, delivering bite-sized mysteries that left listeners hungry for more night after night. Bob Bailey became the definitive voice of Johnny Dollar, a character so perfectly realized that millions of Americans considered him as real as their own neighbors. The show's success lay in its authenticity—real insurance cases inspired the scripts, lending gravitas to every twist and turn. By 1956, Bailey had already made Johnny Dollar unforgettable, and *The Todd Matter* stands as evidence of why this character endured in the public imagination long after radio's prime had faded.

Don't miss your appointment with fate. Settle in, dim the lights, and let Bob Bailey transport you back to an era when radio ruled the airwaves and a man's wits were his greatest asset. Johnny Dollar is waiting.
