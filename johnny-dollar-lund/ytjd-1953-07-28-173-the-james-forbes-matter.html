# Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar: The James Forbes Matter

Picture yourself in a smoky Manhattan office on a sweltering July evening in 1953, as insurance investigator Johnny Dollar closes the case file on one of his most perplexing assignments. When a seemingly straightforward claim turns into a labyrinth of deception and hidden motives, our world-weary protagonist finds himself navigating the shadowy corridors of high finance and darker human impulses. With each clue meticulously documented in his case notes, Dollar pieces together a puzzle where nothing—and no one—is quite what they seem. The Forbes matter promises the kind of taut, intelligent storytelling that made this show an essential appointment for millions of radio listeners, complete with authentic sound design that places you directly into the investigation.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* represented the golden age of radio drama in its finest form. Broadcasting from 1952 to 1955 on CBS, the show distinguished itself through crisp, intelligent scripts and John Lund's perfectly calibrated performance as Dollar—a man caught between his professional cynicism and his fundamental decency. Unlike the pulpier detective shows of the era, Johnny Dollar's cases relied on clever plotting and genuine mystery rather than gunfire and melodrama. Each episode was a self-contained puzzle, meticulously researched and crafted to appeal to listeners who appreciated sophistication alongside suspense.

Don't miss your chance to step into the shoes of radio's most reliable investigator. *The James Forbes Matter* awaits you with the kind of compelling storytelling that defined an era—where a good mystery, a capable detective, and impeccable production values created an unforgettable listening experience. Tune in and discover why Johnny Dollar became a legend of the airwaves.
