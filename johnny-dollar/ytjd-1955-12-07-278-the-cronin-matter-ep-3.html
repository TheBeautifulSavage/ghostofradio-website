# The Cronin Matter - Episode 3

The rain hammers against the windows of a Dublin hotel room as Johnny Dollar finds himself tangled in a web of international deception far more dangerous than any simple insurance claim. In this gripping third installment of *The Cronin Matter*, our quick-witted investigator discovers that the missing heirloom he's been tracking across the Atlantic is merely bait in a deadly game orchestrated by forces he's only beginning to understand. With each carefully worded conversation and shadowy encounter, the noose tightens around Johnny—and he must decide whether to trust the mysterious woman claiming to help him or the desperate men prepared to silence him permanently. The tension crackles through every commercial break as listeners are pulled deeper into a labyrinth of false identities, blackmail, and murder where the real currency isn't dollars, but survival.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* represents the golden age of radio drama at its finest—a show that revitalized the detective genre for the postwar audience by making insurance investigation genuinely compelling. Bob Bailey's portrayal of the freelance investigator struck a chord with 1950s America, transforming mundane claims into psychological thrillers that unfolded across multiple episodes. The Cronin Matter exemplifies the show's mastery of serialized storytelling, building momentum week after week while maintaining the atmospheric noir sensibility that made Johnny Dollar essential listening for millions of Americans gathered around their radio sets every weeknight.

Don't miss this thrilling chapter in one of radio's most beloved mysteries. Tune in to hear how Johnny Dollar navigates the treacherous Irish underworld and whether he'll emerge from Dublin alive—or whether *The Cronin Matter* will finally prove too costly for even the most resourceful investigator in the business.
