# The Nick Shurn Matter – Episode 4

Picture this: December 22nd, 1955. While Americans trim their Christmas trees and prepare for the holidays, private investigator Johnny Dollar sits in a smoky office, nursing cold coffee and nursing colder facts. The Nick Shurn matter has twisted deeper than anyone anticipated—and Episode 4 finds our hero cornered between lies that smell like gin and a conspiracy that reeks of desperation. What started as a routine insurance claim has become a labyrinth of blackmail, hidden identities, and a man who may or may not be who he claims. As the noose tightens and Christmas carols drift through the city streets, Johnny must navigate betrayal and danger with nothing but his wits and his .38. The stakes have never been higher, and the clock is ticking.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* arrived on CBS radio during the golden age of the medium, when millions gathered around their sets each week to follow America's most famous insurance investigator. Created by writer Jack Johnstone and starring Mandel Kramer as the quick-thinking Johnny, the show became legendary for its snappy dialogue, intricate plotting, and that unmistakable 1950s noir sensibility—part detective story, part financial thriller. By 1955, the show had perfected its formula: Johnny's cases were always personal, always dangerous, and always paid by the insurance company. The Nick Shurn Matter represented the show at its creative peak, a four-part serial that showcased everything listeners loved.

Don't miss this gripping chapter in Johnny Dollar's case files. Tune in and discover why *Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* remains radio's most intelligent crime drama—where every clue counts and every decision could be the last one you make.
