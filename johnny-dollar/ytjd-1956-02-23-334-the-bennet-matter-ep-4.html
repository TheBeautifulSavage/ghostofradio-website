Picture yourself in the smoky back room of a Manhattan office building on a gray February evening in 1956. Our man Johnny Dollar is closing in on the truth—or so he thinks—in a tangled web of inheritance claims, forged documents, and a family's darkest secrets. As the fourth installment of The Bennet Matter unfolds, the case that seemed straightforward has become treacherously complex. Witnesses contradict each other, evidence disappears, and Johnny finds himself asking the one question that keeps him awake at night: who's lying, and what are they willing to do to keep their lie buried? With only his wits, his expense account, and his hard-earned instincts, Johnny Dollar navigates the shadowy corridors of greed and deception where every shadow conceals another suspect.

For seven thrilling years, Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar captivated millions of listeners with stories that felt ripped from tomorrow's headlines. By 1956, the show had perfected its craft—offering something rare in the golden age of radio: a protagonist as complicated and human as the mysteries he solved. Unlike the superhero detectives who dominated the airwaves, Johnny was a working man, a professional troubleshooter for the insurance industry who dealt in the mundane currency of human frailty rather than elaborate schemes. The Bennet Matter represents the show at its peak, delivering a multi-episode arc that trusts its audience's intelligence and patience.

Don't miss the pivotal fourth chapter of this masterfully crafted mystery. Tune in and discover why Johnny Dollar remains one of radio's most beloved and enduring characters—a man whose ability to uncover truth in a world of deception made him genuinely, unforgettably yours.
