# The Clinton Matter - Episode 5

The rain hammers against the windowpane as Johnny Dollar sits in a dimly lit hotel room, nursing his third coffee of the night. A case that seemed straightforward forty-eight hours ago has twisted into something far more sinister—corruption, blackmail, and a missing heiress all converge on this fifth and final installment of "The Clinton Matter." As the insurance investigator pieces together the fractured testimony of witnesses, each revelation peels back another layer of deception. Listeners will find themselves trapped in the suffocating tension of a manhunt against the clock, where every decision could mean the difference between solving the case and becoming another body for the city morgue to process.

"Yours Truly Johnny Dollar" stands as a masterpiece of American radio drama, pioneering a format that made the insurance investigator an unlikely noir hero. By 1956, when this episode aired, the show had already captivated millions with its meticulous attention to detail—actual claim numbers, expense reports, and procedural authenticity grounded each mystery in gritty realism. Johnny Dollar, brought to vivid life by various talented voice actors throughout the show's run, represented the everyman detective; not a two-fisted superhero, but an intelligent, world-weary professional navigating moral ambiguity in post-war America. The Clinton Matter serial exemplified the show's evolution toward more complex, multi-part narratives that demanded active listener engagement and rewarded devoted audiences with intricate plotting.

Tune in and experience what millions heard crackling through their radio speakers on this March evening in 1956. "The Clinton Matter - Episode 5" awaits, ready to draw you into a world of shadows, secrets, and the inexorable logic of an investigator who always—eventually—finds the truth.
