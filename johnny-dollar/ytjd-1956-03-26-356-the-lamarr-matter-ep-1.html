# The Lamarr Matter (Episode 1)

Step into the shadowed offices of insurance investigator Johnny Dollar as he takes on a case that reeks of Old Hollywood intrigue and danger. On this March evening in 1956, Dollar finds himself entangled in the mysterious disappearance of a valuable diamond—and a woman's desperate plea for help. The Manhattan skyline glitters in the background as our hero navigates a labyrinth of lies, blackmail, and double-crossing that promises to keep you on the edge of your seat. With each clue Johnny unearths, the stakes grow higher, and you'll hear every footstep, every whispered threat, every tense moment of negotiation. This is a two-part adventure that launches with all the suspense and sophistication that made Johnny Dollar radio's most compelling private investigator.

*Yours Truly Johnny Dollar* stood apart in the golden age of radio drama because it treated its audience like adults. Rather than relying on melodrama or supernatural thrills, the show grounded itself in procedural realism—Johnny was an insurance investigator first, a detective second, and his cases often hinged on financial motive and clever deception rather than gunplay alone. Sponsored by Hallmark Cards, the show developed a devoted following that appreciated its tight scripts, authentic dialogue, and the unflappable persona of star Bob Bailey, who brought a world-weary wisdom and dry humor to every investigation. By 1956, the show had become essential listening for anyone who loved smart crime drama.

Tune in now to hear how Johnny Dollar begins unraveling *The Lamarr Matter*. With danger lurking at every turn and a mystery that demands solving, this is vintage radio storytelling at its finest—a reminder of when drama meant intelligence, atmosphere meant artistry, and a single voice could transport you entirely into another world.
