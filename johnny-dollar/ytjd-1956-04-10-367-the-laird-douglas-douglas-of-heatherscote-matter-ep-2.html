# The Laird Douglas Douglas Of Heatherscote Matter – Part Two

The mist rolls thick over the Scottish highlands as Johnny Dollar finds himself tangled in a web of aristocratic secrets and insurance fraud that stretches from Edinburgh to the American heartland. In this gripping second installment, our intrepid investigator must navigate the treacherous waters of Old World nobility meeting New World ambition, where a missing heir, forged documents, and a suspicious death threaten to unravel a multi-thousand dollar claim. With each suspicious detail, each cryptic conversation with the brooding Laird himself, the tension mounts—what really happened to the Douglas fortune, and why are so many powerful people invested in keeping Johnny from the truth? The crackling transatlantic phone lines and the atmospheric Scottish setting create an intimate sense of danger, as Johnny's trademark wit and dogged determination face their match against centuries of family intrigue.

What made *Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* the gold standard of radio drama was precisely this: its refusal to offer easy answers or convenient morality. Unlike competitors who dealt in heroes and villains, Johnny inhabited a gray world where insurance adjusters must think like detectives, where every witness has a motive, and where protecting a company's interests sometimes meant exposing uncomfortable truths about even the most respectable families. Bob Bailey's Emmy-winning performance as Johnny brought a modern skepticism to each case, supported by talented casts who made even minor witnesses feel like fully realized characters with their own secrets to guard.

Settle into your favorite chair, adjust the dial to find CBS's finest, and prepare yourself for an evening of classic mystery radio. Whether you're a longtime devotee of *Johnny Dollar* or discovering the show for the first time, this two-part Scottish adventure delivers everything that made the program essential listening for millions of Americans during radio's golden age.
