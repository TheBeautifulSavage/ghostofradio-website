# The Tears of Night Matter - Part Five

In the shadowed corners of the insurance world, where claims breed conspiracy and desperation wears a thousand faces, Johnny Dollar finds himself tangled in a case that cuts deeper than mere dollars and cents. As part five of "The Tears of Night Matter" unfolds on this May evening in 1956, our world-weary investigator discovers that some cases demand more than sharp instincts and sharper wits—they demand a reckoning with the very human cost of greed. With each piece of the puzzle clicking into place, the tension mounts toward a climax that promises to shake the foundations of everyone involved. The noir atmosphere crackles with danger; you can almost hear the rain on the windows and feel the weight of secrets pressing down in dimly lit hotel rooms and back-alley meetings.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* stands as a towering achievement in American radio, and this multi-part episode exemplifies why. The show pioneered the serialized insurance investigation format, giving listeners something radio had never quite offered before—a sophisticated adult protagonist navigating morally complicated terrain where right and wrong blur like wet watercolors. Johnny Dollar himself became an icon: the everyman detective with the Hohmeister expense account and an uncommon ability to see through lies. By 1956, the show had already established itself as a favorite among listeners hungry for intelligent crime drama, and episodes like this five-parter showcase the program's gift for weaving intricate narratives that reward careful attention.

Don't miss the climactic fifth installment of this gripping case. Tune in and discover what Johnny Dollar uncovers when the tears of night finally matter.
