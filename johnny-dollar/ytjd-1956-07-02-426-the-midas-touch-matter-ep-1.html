# The Midas Touch Matter – Part One

Step into the shadowy world of expense account detective Johnny Dollar as he takes on a case that glitters with danger and deception. When a wealthy industrialist's golden touch turns to tarnish, Dollar finds himself entangled in a web of corporate espionage, mysterious theft, and threats that reach far beyond the boardroom. This first installment crackles with the unmistakable tension of a man who's learned that in the world of high finance, everyone has something to hide. The crisp narration of John Michael Kane pulls you directly into Dollar's investigation as he begins to unravel a conspiracy that will test his wits, his courage, and his ability to separate fact from carefully constructed lies. From the moment the curtain rises on this episode, you'll feel the weight of suspicion hanging over every conversation—is the industrialist's business partner a trusted confidant or a hidden enemy? What secrets lurk behind closed office doors?

By 1956, *Yours Truly Johnny Dollar* had already established itself as the gold standard of radio detective drama. Unlike the superhero antics of other programs, this show grounded itself in authentic insurance investigation—Johnny Dollar wasn't fighting supervillains, he was pursuing leads, tracking paper trails, and outsmarting clever criminals with his sharp mind and sharper instincts. The multi-part episodes became the show's signature, allowing complex mysteries to unfold with genuine narrative depth, giving listeners something to anticipate each evening and rewarding their loyalty with sophisticated storytelling that respected their intelligence.

Don't miss this captivating two-part adventure. Tune in now and discover why audiences made Johnny Dollar one of radio's most beloved investigators, where every case teaches a new lesson about greed, trust, and the true price of success.
