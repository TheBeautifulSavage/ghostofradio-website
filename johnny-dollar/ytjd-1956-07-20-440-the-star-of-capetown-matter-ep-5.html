# The Star of Capetown Matter – Episode 5

The trail grows colder and more dangerous as Johnny Dollar finds himself tangled in a web of international intrigue that stretches from the glittering diamond exchanges of Cape Town to the shadowed streets of a city he can no longer trust. In this fifth installment of "The Star of Capetown Matter," our weary investigator discovers that the precious gem at the heart of his case carries secrets far more valuable—and far more lethal—than insurance premiums and claim settlements. With nothing but his wits, his expense account, and a mounting sense of dread, Johnny must navigate a labyrinth of double-crosses and desperate characters. The quintet of episodes unfolds like a noir fever dream, each revelation pulling him deeper into a conspiracy where everyone has something to hide and everyone has a motive to silence him. By episode five, the pressure mounts to a fever pitch, and listeners will find themselves gripping their radio sets as the mystery tightens around our protagonist like a noose.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* became CBS's crown jewel of detective programming, a show that elevated the insurance investigator to the status of hard-boiled legend. What set it apart from competitors was its serialized story arcs—multi-part cases that demanded listener loyalty and created genuine narrative momentum. Running from 1949 through 1962, the program proved that radio audiences craved complexity, atmosphere, and the kind of morally ambiguous storytelling that would later define television noir. Mandel Kramer's weary delivery of Johnny's deadpan narration became iconic, a voice for the everyman caught in extraordinary circumstances.

Tune in to *The Star of Capetown Matter* and discover why this masterpiece of audio drama still captivates listeners nearly seventy years later. Where diamonds glitter and danger lurks, Johnny Dollar always answers the call.
