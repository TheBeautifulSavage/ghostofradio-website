# The Crystal Lake Matter – Episode 4

The summer night air hangs thick with suspicion as Johnny Dollar pulls his car up to the exclusive Crystal Lake resort, where a missing jeweler and a tangle of false claims have left insurance investigators scratching their heads. This fourth installment of the Crystal Lake investigation plunges our quick-witted investigator deeper into a web of wealthy suspects, each with motive and opportunity aplenty. You'll hear the crunch of gravel underfoot, the hushed conversations in dimly lit hotel lounges, and the sharp crack of Johnny's accusations as he pieces together the truth from a mountain of contradictions. The tension builds methodically—this is not a show that rushes—as Johnny's famous manila envelope grows thicker with evidence, photographs, and the kinds of mundane details that reveal grand deceptions. By episode's end, you'll understand why audiences tuned in faithfully every week, hanging on every carefully chosen word.

*Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* was a masterclass in American radio drama, distinguished by its meticulous scriptwriting and the brilliant performance of Bob Bailey, whose rapid-fire delivery and natural charm made Johnny the everyman hero of the insurance world. Unlike the supernatural tales or superhero fantasies that dominated radio, this show grounded itself in the real anxieties of post-war America—fraud, theft, and the corruption lurking beneath respectable surfaces. Running for thirteen years on CBS, it proved that listeners craved intelligent mysteries and authentic detective work rendered through the intimate medium of sound. The "matter" episodes like this one showcased the show's serialized storytelling at its finest, allowing complex cases to unfold across multiple nights with genuine narrative momentum.

Don't miss this crucial chapter in the Crystal Lake investigation. Tune in and discover why *Yours Truly, Johnny Dollar* remains one of radio's most beloved programs—a gem of American broadcasting that rewards careful listening.
