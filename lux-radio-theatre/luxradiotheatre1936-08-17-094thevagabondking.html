The story revolves around Count Franz von Stroheim, a charming and cunning nobleman who seeks to reclaim his lost wealth and social standing through any means necessary. As he navigates the treacherous world of high society, the Count's true nature is slowly revealed, exposing him as a master manipulator with a penchant for deception.

The atmosphere in this episode is one of tension and foreboding, with each character's intentions shrouded in mystery. The characters are expertly portrayed by the Lux Radio Theatre cast, with each actor bringing depth and nuance to their respective roles. As the story hurtles towards its dramatic conclusion, the listener is left breathless, eager to see justice served on the deserving Vagabond King.
