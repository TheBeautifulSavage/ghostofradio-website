"The Ex-Mrs. Bradford" is a Lux Radio Theatre production that weaves a complex web of love, betrayal, and redemption. The story centers around Henry Bradley, a wealthy businessman who's struggling to come to terms with the departure of his wife, Eleanor. As he navigates his new life without her, Henry becomes increasingly convinced that Eleanor has moved on and is now living a life of luxury with another man, Bradford himself. This notion sparks a fierce emotional battle within Henry, leading him down a path of revenge and self-destruction. The atmosphere in this episode is tense and foreboding, punctuated by moments of dark humor and wit.

At the heart of this drama are the complex characters that make up the Bradley household. Henry's inner turmoil is palpable, while Eleanor's transformation from loving wife to calculating ex-wife is both captivating and unsettling. As the episode hurtles towards its climax, listeners are left wondering what lies in store for these characters and whether they'll find redemption or remain trapped in their destructive cycles.

The Lux Radio Theatre's masterful storytelling and talented voice cast bring this episode to life with remarkable fidelity. With its thought-provoking themes and expertly crafted characters, "The Ex-Mrs. Bradford" remains a gripping and unsettling listen even today.
