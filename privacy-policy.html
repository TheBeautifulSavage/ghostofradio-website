Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Ghost of Radio ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our website at ghostofradio.com and our Facebook application ("Ghost of Radio" app). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

About Our Facebook Application

The Ghost of Radio Facebook application is used solely to publish content to our own Facebook Page. The app is used by the page administrators to post updates, share episodes, and manage content on the Ghost of Radio Facebook Page.

What the App Does

  • Posts content (text, links, images) to the Ghost of Radio Facebook Page
  • Manages and schedules posts on our own Facebook Page
  • Reads insights and analytics for our own Facebook Page

What the App Does NOT Do

  • Does not collect, store, or process any user data from Facebook users
  • Does not access any third-party user profiles, friend lists, or personal information
  • Does not track, monitor, or collect data from visitors to our Facebook Page
  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not use any data for advertising, analytics beyond our own page, or any commercial purpose involving user data

Data Collection

Website (ghostofradio.com)

Our website is a static informational site. We do not:

  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Collect personal information
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Process payments or financial information
  • Use analytics services that collect personal data

Our website may embed YouTube videos. When you play an embedded video, YouTube's own privacy policy applies to any data collected by YouTube. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Facebook Application

The Ghost of Radio Facebook application operates exclusively on our own Facebook Page. It accesses only the permissions necessary to post content to and manage our own Page. No user data is collected, stored, or processed through the application.

Data Storage and Security

Since we do not collect user data, there is no user data to store or secure. Our Facebook application credentials and page access tokens are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized administrators of Ghost of Radio.

Third-Party Services

Our website and services interact with the following third-party platforms:

  • YouTube — for hosting and embedding our radio show episodes
  • Facebook / Meta — for our Facebook Page and the Ghost of Radio application
  • Google Fonts — for web typography on our website

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no user data to delete. If you believe we have inadvertently collected any information about you, please contact us and we will promptly investigate and address the matter.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
