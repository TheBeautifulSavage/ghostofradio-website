# The Railroad Hour: "I Married an Angel"

Step aboard for an enchanting evening of music and romance as *The Railroad Hour* presents Rodgers and Hart's delightful musical comedy, "I Married an Angel." Picture yourself in the warm glow of your radio on this winter's evening—the orchestra strikes up with ethereal charm as we follow a worldly banker who discovers that his bride is quite literally an angel sent from heaven. What unfolds is a whimsical collision between the celestial and the mundane, punctuated by some of the most beautiful melodies ever composed. You'll hear soaring vocal performances that capture both the heavenly wonder of angelic love and the very earthly complications that ensue when a mortal dares to marry someone from above. The talented cast brings genuine warmth and humor to this tale, while the lush orchestration transports listeners to a realm where the impossible becomes wonderfully real.

*The Railroad Hour* stands as one of radio's most beloved musical dramas, and this particular adaptation showcases why the series captured America's heart during the postwar years. Rodgers and Hart's original 1938 Broadway hit provides perfect material for radio's intimate medium, where music and storytelling intertwine seamlessly. The show's commitment to presenting complete Broadway scores—performed by seasoned professionals—set it apart from typical radio fare and made it essential listening for music lovers across the nation.

Don't miss this celestial confection. Tune in to experience the magic that made *The Railroad Hour* America's premier musical drama, where every episode promised Broadway glamour delivered straight into your living room. This is radio at its most enchanting.
