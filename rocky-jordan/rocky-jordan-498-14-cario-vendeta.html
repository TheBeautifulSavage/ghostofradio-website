Picture yourself in a smoke-filled Cairo nightclub as the call to evening prayer echoes across the Nile—this is where our hero Rocky Jordan finds himself ensnared in a web of revenge that threatens to consume the entire city. When a mysterious figure from the past emerges from the shadows with blood in their eyes, Rocky must navigate the labyrinthine streets and back alleys of Egypt's capital, where every stranger could be an ally or an assassin. The tension crackles through your radio speaker as gunfire erupts in hidden bazaars, coded messages are exchanged in whispered conversations, and Rocky's quick thinking becomes his only weapon against an enemy who knows his every move. This episode pulses with the exotic danger and moral complexity that made listeners across America tune in week after week, eager to follow their favorite globe-trotting adventurer into another perilous situation.

Rocky Jordan's ten-year run on CBS represented the golden age of adventure radio drama, when exotic locales and hard-boiled protagonists could transport audiences far beyond their living rooms. Created by and starring Jack Moyles, the show captured post-war America's fascination with international intrigue and the American everyman succeeding through courage and cunning in foreign lands. Cairo became as much a character as Rocky himself—a city of ancient mysteries colliding with modern danger, where American ingenuity proved superior to Old World vengeance.

For devotees of classic radio adventure and newcomers alike, "Cairo Vendetta" offers everything that made Rocky Jordan essential listening: sharp dialogue, mounting suspense, and a hero who wins through intelligence and determination rather than mere luck. Tune in and experience why millions huddled around their radios for this unforgettable episode.
