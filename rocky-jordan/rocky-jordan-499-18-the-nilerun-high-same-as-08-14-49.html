# Rocky Jordan - "The Nilerun High"

Picture yourself settled into your favorite armchair on a warm evening in 1949, the amber glow of your radio dial cutting through the darkness as the iconic theme swells. You're about to follow Rocky Jordan—that sharp-witted American adventurer operating from his base in Cairo—into the shadowy underworld of the Nile where a luxury riverboat named the *Nilerun* holds secrets as deep as the ancient river itself. As the fog horn sounds and the waves lap against the dock, Rocky finds himself entangled in a dangerous scheme aboard this floating palace of intrigue. With nothing but his quick wits, trusty sidekick Beaumont, and an eye for trouble, Rocky must navigate a treacherous web of smugglers, con artists, and mysterious passengers before the *Nilerun* sails into deadly waters. Every shadow could conceal a threat; every elegant passenger might be a criminal in disguise. The tension builds minute by minute as Rocky pieces together a puzzle that grows more lethal with each revelation.

*Rocky Jordan* captured the imagination of millions during radio's golden age by transporting listeners to exotic locales—a far cry from the mundane world of wartime and postwar America. The show's success lay in its ability to blend hardboiled detective storytelling with globe-trotting adventure, all while maintaining the intimate connection only radio could provide. Broadcast live from studios across the country, each episode offered an escape hatch to danger, romance, and intrigue in distant lands, starring the dependable Jack Moyles as our hero and featuring a cast of colorful supporting characters.

Don't miss this thrilling chapter in Rocky's Cairo chronicles. Whether you're a devoted fan who remembers hearing this episode live or discovering Rocky Jordan's adventures for the first time, *"The Nilerun High"* promises the classic blend of wit, danger, and international intrigue that made this show essential listening.
