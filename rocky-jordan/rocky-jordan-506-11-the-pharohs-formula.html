In this thrilling installment of Rocky Jordan, our intrepid American detective finds himself ensnared in a web of ancient mystery and modern murder in the shadow of Cairo's legendary pyramids. When a priceless alchemical formula—rumored to grant unimaginable wealth and power—surfaces in the hands of a ruthless black marketeer, Rocky must navigate the treacherous streets of post-war Egypt, dodging both corrupt officials and shadowy criminals who would kill to possess the secret. The stakes escalate when the body of a prominent archaeologist is discovered, the formula vanishes, and Rocky's trusted associate Hoagy finds himself framed for the crime. With tension mounting and time running out, our hero must race against the clock to uncover the truth buried beneath layers of deception, while danger lurks around every corner in the exotic, intoxicating atmosphere of the Nile delta.

Rocky Jordan's appeal lay in its sophisticated blend of globe-trotting adventure and hard-boiled detective work, offering listeners an escape into exotic locales that felt both thrillingly dangerous and tantalizingly real. Broadcast during radio's golden age when audiences huddled around their sets for weekly serials, the show distinguished itself through snappy dialogue, authentic location research, and lead Jack Moyles's charismatic performance as the unflappable Jordan. Episodes like "The Pharaoh's Formula" exemplified why the series captivated millions—combining the allure of archaeological mystery with the gritty realism of contemporary crime fiction.
