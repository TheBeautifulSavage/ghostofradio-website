When Rocky Jordan checks into the Hotel Semirami in Cairo, he expects another routine night in the exotic capital of Egypt. Instead, he finds himself entangled in a mystery that will test every ounce of his cunning and courage. Dr. Markon, a brilliant but haunted scientist, arrives at the hotel with something precious—a discovery that powerful forces will stop at nothing to possess. As Rocky peels back layers of intrigue involving shadowy operatives and dangerous secrets, the stakes mount with each passing moment. What exactly has Dr. Markon found, and why are men willing to kill for it? With his quick wits, his fists, and the help of his faithful sidekick Zeke Cobb, Rocky must navigate the treacherous streets of Cairo and uncover the truth before nightfall brings deadly reckoning.

Rocky Jordan captivated millions of American listeners during the golden age of radio, offering audiences an escape to glamorous and dangerous locales across the globe. Starring John Payne as the resourceful title character, the show brilliantly captured the postwar appetite for adventure and sophistication, blending the hard-boiled detective genre with international intrigue. Set against the backdrop of the late 1940s, Rocky Jordan became a beloved fixture on CBS radio, showcasing exotic locales that few Americans could visit in person but could vividly imagine through master sound design and compelling storytelling.
