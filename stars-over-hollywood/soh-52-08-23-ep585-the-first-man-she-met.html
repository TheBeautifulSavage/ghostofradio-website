Stars Over Hollywood captured the golden age of radio drama at its peak, bringing the allure and danger of the film industry into living rooms across America during the early 1940s. With top-tier talent from both stage and screen, the show crafted sophisticated narratives that appealed to the aspirations of Depression and wartime audiences who craved both escapism and authenticity.
