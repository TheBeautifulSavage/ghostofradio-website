Studio One stands as one of CBS's most ambitious dramatic anthology series, a showcase for Broadway-caliber talent and scripts that refuse to be confined by genre or convention. Airing live from New York's Columbia Broadcasting studios, each episode represents a completely different story, allowing writers to explore the full spectrum of human experience—from the mundane to the miraculous. By May 1948, the show had established itself as essential listening, with critics and devoted fans alike praising its willingness to take risks and its stunning casts of emerging talent. "Angelic Avengers" exemplifies this fearless approach, blending the metaphysical with the dramatic in ways that challenge both heart and mind.
