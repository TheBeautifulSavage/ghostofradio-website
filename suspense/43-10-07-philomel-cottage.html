Moreover, the war effort had brought about significant changes in American culture, including increased censorship and propaganda efforts. Radio drama played a crucial role in shaping public opinion and providing entertainment for soldiers and civilians alike. With its carefully crafted narratives and clever editing, Suspense was uniquely positioned to navigate this shifting landscape.
