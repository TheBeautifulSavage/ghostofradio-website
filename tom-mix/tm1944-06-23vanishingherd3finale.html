# The Vanishing Herd, Part Three: The Finale

As our final installment of "The Vanishing Herd" crackles to life on your speaker this June evening in 1944, Tom Mix faces his darkest hour yet. Somewhere in the vast badlands of Montana, a ruthless rustling operation has stripped an honest rancher of his entire herd—and now the trail has gone cold. With only his wits, his trusty horse Tony, and the loyal support of the Ralston Straight Shooters, Tom must uncover the mastermind behind the theft before the evidence vanishes forever. Expect tense confrontations in shadowy bunkhouses, desperate chases across treacherous terrain, and the kind of moral reckoning that has made this character an American icon. The crackling sound effects—galloping hooves, six-shooters, and the whistle of wind through canyon walls—transport you directly to the frontier, where justice hangs in the balance.

For over a decade, Tom Mix and the Ralston Straight Shooters have captivated millions of American listeners, from schoolchildren to their parents gathered around the radio set. In 1944, with the nation's young men fighting overseas, these adventures offer more than mere escapism; they represent enduring American values of honesty, courage, and fair dealing. The show's partnership with Ralston Purina has made it a staple of breakfast tables across the country, while the real Tom Mix—a former Wild West show performer and early cinema star—lends authentic frontier credibility to every episode.

Don't miss this thrilling conclusion! Tune in tonight to discover whether Tom Mix can restore honor to a desperate man and prove that right ultimately prevails, no matter how cunningly evil schemes itself. This is radio adventure at its finest.
