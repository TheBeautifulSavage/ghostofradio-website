EPISODE SUMMARY:

In "How to", a gripping episode of X Minus One, a young woman named Karen finds herself in the midst of a surreal and terrifying situation. After being brutally raped and left for dead on a remote highway, Karen discovers that her assailant has not only left her with physical scars but also with a disturbing fascination with her. As she delves deeper into the mystery of her attacker's intentions, Karen becomes entangled in a dark world where the boundaries between reality and fantasy are blurred.

The atmosphere is tense and foreboding, with a sense of impending doom that permeates every scene. The sound design, expertly crafted by X Minus One's audio team, adds to the unease, with creaking doors, shattering glass, and an eerie score that seems to pulse with Karen's growing fear. The cast, led by actress Lois Lane, delivers a masterclass performance, bringing depth and nuance to their characters.

As the episode unfolds, the listener is drawn into a world of psychological manipulation and cat-and-mouse games, where Karen must confront her attacker in a desperate bid for survival. Will she be able to outsmart him, or will she become his next victim? "How to" is a masterclass in suspense storytelling, keeping listeners on the edge of their seats until the very end.
